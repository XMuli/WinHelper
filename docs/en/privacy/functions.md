## Defender Switch
Quickly enable or disable Microsoft Defender Antivirus (run as admin).  
**Modes**  
- Stop: Defender off; no scans.  
- Start: Windows manages Defender; if another AV is present, Defender is disabled.  
- Enable: Defender runs regardless of other AV.
